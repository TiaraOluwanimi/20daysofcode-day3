The App About

Hate stress, love food and socializing with friends?
We do, too!

TheFoodApp was started by a handful of food-obsessed friends who, like you, start thinking about what’s for dinner shortly after lunchtime, believe meals are best shared with loved ones, and are pretty sure social events that aren’t all about food should be considered a faux pas. We believe in the power of food to tell stories, enrich our daily lives, deepen our relationships with friends and family, and forge connections with the world around us. Plus, we are notorious for snapping pictures of each dish we eat before taking the first bite.

The result is TheFoodApp: the social food-and-friends app that makes it easy for you to order food from your favorite restaurants and have, it delivered to your doorstep, host and attend food events, and share photos of your food experiences as they happen. SupperClub is all about eating well and building community -- in person, in the kitchen, and around the table

Our app is perfect for pop-up restaurants, or just individuals who want to connect with friends and share their favorite food adventures. The mobile app will be available for iOS on July 10th, and an Android release slated for the summer of 2020. Won’t you join us at TheFoodApp?
